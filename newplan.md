# PRD: Cabana MVP + Landing Page Flow

## 1. Product

**Cabana** is an autonomous-feeling AI business builder that turns a rough internet business idea into a live "First Sale Crew."

The user enters an idea, and Cabana spins up 6 agents that immediately start researching, shaping the offer, building the page, drafting outreach, creating content, and tracking revenue signals.

The goal is not to promise passive income. The goal is:

> **Get users from idea to first real revenue signal.**

A revenue signal can be:

* first sale
* payment click
* waitlist signup
* interested reply
* booked call
* pre-order
* strong customer feedback

Cabana is for internet business hopefuls who have tried or considered:

* dropshipping
* Shopify
* Whop
* Gumroad
* vibe-coded SaaS
* paid communities
* affiliate sites
* faceless YouTube/TikTok

Their pain:

> "I have ideas, but I never get to the point where real people can buy."

## 4. MVP wedge

The MVP should be:

# **Autonomous First Sale Crew**

Not a generic agent platform.
Not a full company builder.
Not an educational course.

Cabana should feel like:

> "I gave it an idea and suddenly 6 agents were working on my business."

## 5. Core product loop

The repeatable loop:

> **Idea → crew works → assets appear → user approves → market test runs → signals come back → crew adapts → repeat**

The app should constantly push the user toward external validation.

The key product question:

> What is the next best play to get closer to revenue?

* competitor notes
* niche communities

**Job:** turn the idea into a sellable offer.

* CTA
* FAQ

**Job:** interpret signals and choose next plays.

* bottlenecks

## 13. Backend logic

### Main generation flow

When user submits idea:

1. Create Cabana record
2. Create 6 agent records
3. Run first-pass generation for each agent
4. Save outputs
5. Display live statuses
6. Generate combined launch recommendation
7. Show free preview
8. Trigger upgrade CTA

### Agent outputs can be generated sequentially for MVP

Order:

1. Strategist creates offer angles
2. Scout creates market research
3. Builder creates page from selected/recommended offer
4. Seller creates outreach
5. Creator creates content
6. Analyst creates next plan

The UI can make this feel parallel.

## 16. MVP success criteria

Good early signal:

* 40%+ of visitors who submit an idea reach preview
* 20%+ click "Launch this Cabana"
* 5–10% pay or start trial
* 25%+ of paid users publish/copy their page
* 25%+ approve/copy outreach
* 10%+ log an external signal

The biggest validation is:

> Users take Cabana outputs outside the app.

## 17. Product principles

1. **Motion beats explanation.**
   Show agents working before explaining everything.

2. **Revenue signals beat task completion.**
   Cabana is not a productivity app.

3. **Autonomous-feeling, human-approved.**
   The crew works inside the app, but the user approves external actions.

4. **First sale is the wedge.**
   The long-term vision is a tiny internet business operating system.

5. **Do not sound like a guru.**
   Avoid guaranteed income, passive income, or "retire overnight" claims.

## 18. Final MVP definition

The first shippable version of Cabana is:

> A landing page where users enter an internet business idea, watch 6 AI agents spin up, receive a free business preview, and upgrade to launch a 7-day First Sale Sprint with a generated page, outreach, content, daily plays, and revenue signal tracking.

That's the product.
